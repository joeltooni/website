About This Visualization

This tool helps you understand the pinhole camera model and how 3D world coordinates are projected onto a 2D image plane. Explore concepts like:

  • Intrinsic camera parameters (focal length, principal point)
  • Extrinsic camera parameters (rotation, translation)
  • Projection matrices
  • Field of view and image formation
